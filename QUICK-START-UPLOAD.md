# 🚀 Quick Start - Alumni Database Excel Upload

## In 3 Steps

### 1️⃣ Download Template

Click **"Excel Template"** button in Upload Alumni Data section

- File: `alumni-import-template.xlsx`
- Includes sample data + instructions

### 2️⃣ Add Your Data

Fill in alumni information (required fields + optional details)

### 3️⃣ Upload & Done!

Click **"Upload & Process"** → Data appears instantly

---

## 📋 What Goes in Each Column

```
Required (Must fill):
├── Name: John Doe
├── Email: john@example.com
├── Graduation Year: 2020
└── Branch: CS (or IT, ENTC, ECE, AIDS)

Optional (Can leave empty):
├── Phone: 9876543210
├── Position: Senior Engineer
├── Company: Tech Corp
├── Location: Mumbai
├── LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/...
├── Gender: Male/Female
└── Higher Studies: MS Computer Science
```

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Wrong**: john (incomplete name)
✅ **Right**: John Doe

❌ **Wrong**: john@example (incomplete email)
✅ **Right**: john.doe@example.com

❌ **Wrong**: 20 (incomplete year)
✅ **Right**: 2020

❌ **Wrong**: computer science (branch as text)
✅ **Right**: CS

❌ **Wrong**: Saved as .xls
✅ **Right**: Save as .xlsx
